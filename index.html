Why This Matters

Fake feedback is a business risk, not a moderation problem

AI-generated and fraudulent reviews erode trust, skew decisions, and create regulatory exposure.

01
Regulatory pressure is accelerating
EU Omnibus Directive (2019/2161) mandates verified authenticity. Fines up to 4% of annual revenue or €2M, whichever is higher. Enforced since May 28, 2022.
02
AI makes fraud effortless
AI-generated reviews on TripAdvisor grew 137% between 2019 and 2024, from 4.49% to 10.7% of all reviews. Legacy moderation cannot keep up.
03
Trust compounds
Platforms with trusted ratings outperform on retention, conversion, and partner confidence. Booking.com alone hosts 240+ million verified reviews.
